# Trading Routine

## Overview

Consistent trading requires consistent process. Your routine is the structure that protects you from [[emotional-control]] issues, ensures proper [[risk-management]], and builds the discipline needed for long-term success.

## Pre-Session Routine

### Market Assessment (15-30 minutes before session)

**1. Market Overview**
- Major indices: Up, down, flat?
- Key levels: Support, resistance on daily/weekly charts
- Volatility: VIX reading, ATR levels
- News events: Earnings, economic data, Fed meetings

**2. Watchlist Review**
- Your setup criteria: Check each watchlist item
- Recent price action: What's it doing now?
- Key levels: Entry, stop, target zones
- [[risk-reward-ratio]] calculation

**3. Session Goals**
- What setup am I looking for?
- Max number of trades today (e.g., 3)
- Acceptable risk (always 1%)
- Session focus: Quality over quantity

### Mental Preparation (5-10 minutes)

**1. Emotional Check**
- Rate emotional state 1-10
- If not 4-7: No trading today
- What's my mindset? Calm? Anxious? Overconfident?

**2. Visualization**
- Visualize executing trades correctly
- Visualize taking losses gracefully
- Visualize sticking to plan

**3. Physical State**
- Rested? Fatigued affects decisions
- Distractions? Phone off, focus mode on
- Environment? Quiet, good setup

## During Session Routine

### Trade Execution Checklist

Before every trade, check:

**[ ] Setup Present**
- Does this match my defined setup?
- Have I seen this setup work before?
- What's the win rate on this setup?

**[ ] Risk Defined**
- Where's my stop loss?
- How much am I risking?
- Is it within my 1% rule?

**[ ] Entry Plan**
- Where's my entry trigger?
- Is there a confirmation signal?
- Am I chasing or letting setup come to me?

**[ ] Target Plan**
- Where's my profit target?
- What's my [[risk-reward-ratio]]?
- When will I take partial profits?

**[ ] [[Position-Sizing]] Calculated**
- Based on risk and stop loss
- Account value × Risk% ÷ Stop distance
- Not exceeding any position limits

### Trade Management

**Entry:**
- Execute at planned price
- Immediately set stop loss order
- Set profit target alerts
- Log entry details

**While in trade:**
- Watch price action, don't obsess
- Follow your plan, don't second-guess
- If emotions spike → 30-second pause
- Stick to [[stop-loss-strategies]]

**Exit:**
- Execute at planned exit
- Don't move stops against trade
- Take profits when target hit
- Don't get greedy or fearful

### Session Management

**Trade Count Limit:**
- Set daily max (e.g., 3 trades)
- After hitting max → Stop for session
- No matter what happens → No more trades

**Loss Limit:**
- After 2 consecutive losses → Stop
- Re-evaluate, take break
- Come back fresh or next day

**Win Streak Protocol:**
- After 3+ wins → Reduce size 25%
- Be extra vigilant about [[risk-management]]
- Overconfidence = Big loss coming

## Post-Session Routine

### Trade Review (15-30 minutes)

**For Each Trade:**
```
Date | Setup | Entry | Exit | P/L | Followed Plan? | Notes
```

**Questions to ask:**
1. Did I follow my [[trading-routine]]?
2. Was my [[position-sizing]] correct?
3. How did I feel during the trade?
4. What triggered my emotions?
5. What did I learn?

### Session Assessment

**Quantitative:**
- Number of trades taken
- Win/loss ratio
- Total P/L
- Risk-adjusted return

**Qualitative:**
- How was my discipline?
- Did I force any trades?
- [[emotional-control]] quality?
- Process vs outcome focus?

### Journal Entry

Write about:
- What went well today
- What didn't go well
- What triggered emotional decisions
- What will I do differently tomorrow

Track patterns over weeks and months.

## Weekly Routine

### Sunday Preparation

**1. Weekly Market Analysis**
- Major indices weekly charts
- Key weekly levels
- Sector rotation analysis
- Economic calendar for week

**2. Watchlist Update**
- Remove non-performing symbols
- Add new setups
- Update levels on all watchlist items

**3. Weekly Goals**
- What setup am I focusing on?
- What weakness am I working on?
- What's my weekly risk budget?

### Friday Review

**1. Performance Summary**
- Weekly P/L
- Number of trades
- Win rate by setup type
- Best/worst trades

**2. Discipline Score**
- How many trades followed plan?
- How many forced trades?
- [[risk-management]] compliance?

**3. Lessons Learned**
- What patterns emerged?
- What emotional triggers?
- What to focus on next week?

## Monthly Routine

### Monthly Deep Review

**1. Month-Over-Month Analysis**
- Compare this month to last month
- Are you improving?
- What metrics changed?

**2. Setup Performance**
- Which setups work best?
- Which setups should you drop?
- Win rate by setup type?

**3. System Review**
- Is your [[risk-management]] working?
- Are [[position-sizing]] rules optimal?
- Any needed adjustments?

**4. Emotional Patterns**
- What emotions caused mistakes?
- Which months were disciplined?
- What triggered emotional decisions?

## Common Mistakes in Routines

### 1. Skipping Routine

"I don't have time for routine" → You don't have time to lose money
"Market's moving fast, I'll skip analysis" → That's when mistakes happen
"I know what I'm doing" → Overconfidence kills

**Solution:** Routine is non-negotiable.

### 2. Inconsistent Routine

Sometimes you do it, sometimes you don't
Partial checklist instead of full
Rushed analysis instead of thorough

**Solution:** Same routine, every session, no exceptions.

### 3. Ignoring Emotional State

Trading when angry, sad, or overexcited
Not checking emotional temperature
Forcing trades despite bad state

**Solution:** If emotional state is off → No trading today.

### 4. Not Learning from Mistakes

Making same mistakes repeatedly
Not journaling or reviewing
Blaming market instead of analyzing

**Solution:** Honest self-reflection, continuous improvement.
