# Momentum Trading

## Overview

Momentum trading capitalizes on sustained price moves. The trend is your friend — trade in its direction, not against it. When greed drives momentum, [[emotional-control]] is crucial to avoid chasing.

## Core Principle

**Price that moves up tends to keep moving up.**

Until it doesn't.

## Key Indicators

- **Moving Averages:** 20/50 EMA crossovers
- **MACD:** Positive, rising
- **RSI:** Above 50, not overbought
- **Volume:** High on up moves

## Setup

### 1. Trend Confirmation

- Price above 50-day EMA
- Higher highs and higher lows
- Bullish momentum indicators

### 2. Entry Signal

- Pullback to 20 EMA
- Hold and bounce
- Volume supports bounce

### 3. Risk Management

- Stop below swing low
- [[risk-reward-ratio]]: 2:1 to 3:1
- Follow [[stop-loss-strategies]]

## Common Mistakes

- Chasing extended moves (see [[fear-and-greed]])
- Ignoring trend reversals
- Poor [[position-sizing]] in momentum
- No exit strategy

See [[market-psychology]] for momentum phase psychology.
